Family

and my mother is a housewife as wll as she do farming too, so we were always encouraged to prioritize education and helping others.

Despite the occasional arguments and disagreements, we have always been there for each other and supported one another through the ups and downs of life.

I am grateful for my family and the positive influence they have had on my life.
