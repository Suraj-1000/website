Travel

On this page, you can learn more about my Travel Experience.

I have been traveling for almost 6 years now.

Whenever I get a chance, I love to explore new places and experience different cultures.

So far, I have been fortunate enough to travel to many famous places in Nepal such as Pokhara, Chitwan, Lumbini, and Nagarkot, Sirubari, Bandipur, Ghandruk, Kathmandu.

Travelling is one of my biggest passions, and I always look forward to my next adventure. I believe that exploring new places and meeting new people is one of the best ways to learn and grow as a person.
